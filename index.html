üìñ User Guide

1. Upload QR Code

Click or drag & drop a QR code image into the scan area. The tool supports JPG, PNG, GIF, and BMP formats.

2. Use Camera

Click "Use Camera" to scan QR codes directly from your device's camera. This works best on mobile devices.

3. Review Results

The tool will analyze the QR code content and provide a risk assessment with detailed security information.

4. Safety First

Never scan QR codes from untrusted sources. This tool helps identify potential threats before you access the content.

5. Risk Levels

Safe: 80-100% score
Medium: 50-79% score
High Risk: 0-49% score

6. Copy & Check

Always manually type or copy URLs to verify them before visiting, especially for banking or sensitive accounts.
